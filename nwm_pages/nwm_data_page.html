About

The NWM pages on this site were developed to visualize modeled results from NOAA's experimental National Water Model. The data hosted here is not an official NOAA forecast and plotted data has been altered for research purposes. The webpages included in the Monitoring Areas below display 'interpreted' results that have been altered for use in research investigating the hydrodynamic response of areas impacted by Riverine and Coastal flooding mechanisms. If you have questions or comments please contact the Mason Flood Hazards Research Lab.
